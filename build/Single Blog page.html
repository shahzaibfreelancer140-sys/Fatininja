Wide bottompaper bags

Advantages of wide-bottom paper bags from MarioPack

Paper bags with a wide bottom are an ideal solution for companies that need durable and capacious packaging. Thanks to their construction, these bags are perfect for carrying larger, heavier items such as boxes, food products, books or textiles. The wide bottom provides additional stability and allows for safe storage and transport of a variety of items.

Perfect forcateringand transporting heavy items

Our wide-bottom paper bags are particularly popular in the catering industry. Thanks to their capacity and strength, they are perfect for transporting catering products, food boxes, cakes and other confectionery. These bags are also suitable for retail, where they can be used to pack clothing, shoes, home accessories and office supplies.

Ecological and functional paper bags

Our wide-bottom paper bags are made of high-quality kraft paper, which is not only durable but also ecological. Kraft paper is resistant to mechanical damage, and its biodegradability makes it an excellent choice for companies that care about the environment. Paper bags from MarioPack also have a PZH certificate, which means that they can be safely used in contact with food.
